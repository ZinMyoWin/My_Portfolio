Enomy-Finances

Used Cars Sales Portal

The project involves developing a training package for the adoption of Risk-Based Testing (RBT) in systems development, focusing on a "Used Cars Sales Portal" to demonstrate RBT's benefits and evaluate its outcomes, with distinct functionalities for Users and Administrators.

The project aims to develop an application for MerryMeal, a charitable organization, to provide hot meals to eligible adults while enabling member and caregiver registration, partner and volunteer engagement, fundraising, menu planning, meal delivery management, food safety monitoring, needs reassessment, and a robust Management Information System.
