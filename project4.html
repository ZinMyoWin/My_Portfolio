The MerryMeal project is a charitable initiative providing hot noon meals to eligible adults who cannot cook for themselves due to age, illness, or disability. The application streamlines aspects such as member and caregiver registration, partners and volunteers registration, fundraising, menu planning and preparation, meal delivery management, food safety management, reassessment of need evaluation, and a Management Information System (MIS).

The goal is to create an efficient system to meet the nutritional needs of these adults, enhancing their quality of life through reliable support.

Register

We separated user roles and they have their own authorization to the website. There are 4 users roles; Admin, Partner, Volunteer and Member. All the user roles except the admin need to register and wait for the admin approvals.

Admin Login

We separated user roles and they have their own authorization to the website. Admin is capable of managing the users registration, and approving the registered meals.

Member Login

Member role is capable of viewing the meals, ordering meals, and requesting the caregivers. They can also donate to support the MerryMeal organization.

Partner Login

Partner role is capable of donating, and registering the meals. After registering the meals they need to wait for the admin approvals.

Volunteer Login

Volunteers role is capable of donating, tracking the ordered meals, the requested caregivers.
