The project is for a Risk-Based Testing (RBT) training program for a Used Cars Sales Portal. The portal has two types of users: regular users and administrators. Regular users can register, login, post cars for sale, update their profile, and bid on cars. Administrators can register, login, view registered users, manage car posts, and update their profile. Both user types can visit the home page, view car listings, search for cars, and visit the About Us and Contact Us pages. The training program will guide on the benefits and implementation of RBT for this portal.

The goal of the project is to design and implement a Risk-Based Testing (RBT) training program for a Used Cars Sales Portal. The training program aims to demonstrate the adoption of RBT in the development of the portal, focusing on the different functions that users and administrators can perform. The ultimate objective is to ensure that the system is robust, secure, and provides a seamless experience for all users. This will be achieved by identifying potential risks in the system and prioritizing testing efforts accordingly to mitigate these risks.
